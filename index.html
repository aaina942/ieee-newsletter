Important Announcements for Launchpad Kerala Participants

Dear Participant,

We are excited to invite you to the next stage of our selection process.

Online Aptitude Test Details:

The online aptitude test is scheduled for July 4th and is mandatory for all candidates proceeding to the interview round. Your performance in this test and the technical challenge will determine your final ranking. Interviews will be held in Trivandrum and Kochi based on this ranking.

Mock Test Preparation:

A mock test will be conducted on July 3rd. Further details will be shared soon.

Eligibility Criteria:

Only candidates who have completed at least one task in the Technical Challenge by July 1st are eligible for the aptitude test. If you haven't started the challenge yet, please begin as soon as possible. Detailed instructions are available here.
